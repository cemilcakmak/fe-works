Pizza

Description

A New-York Style white pizza with drizzled olive oil, mozzarella, parmesan, and ricotta cheese with Italian herbs.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Ready-to-Bake Refrigerated Pizza Dough
  • 3 Tablespoons Olive Oil
  • Garlic Powder
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Start with ready-to-bake refrigerated pizza dough.
  2. Top with freshly grated mozzarella.
  3. In a small bowl, stir together ricotta cheese, Parmesan Cheese and salt.
  4. Generously sprinkle with Italian herbs.
  5. Bake at high heat -- 450 degrees to allow the crust to crisp on the bottom.
